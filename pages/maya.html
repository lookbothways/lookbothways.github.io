ITV: 'Primeval'

I was the technical lead on 'Primeval' for Framestore's longform dept. I lead the TD's in look-dev, skinning/muscle, lighting and 3D effects using Mental Ray. I worked on several creatures as well as sucessfully managing the workload amongst the crew. I drew on my commercials and TV experience and got the job done to a very high standard.
BBC / Discovery Channel: 'Oceans'

I'm was a TD in Framestore-CFC's Longform department on the BBC/Discovery docu-drama that followed the life of a Sperm Whale. It featured unfilmable sequences, such as Bull male fights, encounters with Orcas and Giant Squid. We created 4 fully CG underwater environments and creatures for over 400 shots in 6 months (not in showreel .avi).
Jim Henson: 'The Water Giant'

I was the submarine and seabed TD for the entire 'pod rescue' sequence - work included texturing, lighting, pipeline and look development . I completed a 36 shot film-res sequence with one other lighter and compositor. Most shots were lit using Mental Ray. I lit my 'bad sub' shots using Renderman.
