Q: What happens when I'm not at work and not sleeping or eating?
A: Since I don't watch television I do other things. I sometimes attempt to speak Czech with my wife.
Arts
I love traditional animation, Especially Princess Mononoke, Ghost in the shell & Metropolis though I'm not a huge Manga fan these films stand out. I laugh at The Far Side and Calvin and Hobbes and when I have the time I enjoy drawing. I like impressionism, especially Cézanne and Modigliani. I also enjoy Mucha's work in small doses.

Top cartoonist: Bill Watterson
Top cartoon: Dexter's Lab
Literature
I read a lot, enjoyed Ask the Dust and Remains of the day. I have read a lot of beat generation 'classics' - Zen and the art of motorcycle maintenance is still a favourite, though sad. I'm a big fan of science and trivia, Assimov's 'New guide to science' was essential teenage digest. Recently enjoyed Philip Pullman's Dark Materials trilogy.

Top authors: Aldus Huxley, Issac Assimov.
Cinema
I recently invested in a digital projector and sit eight feet from a huge screen. Monster's Inc. looks amazing. Since I set it up I'm increasingly frustrated by everything an audience does if it's not watching the screen quietly. I watch a lot of movies.

Nearly top films, in no particular order: Monsters Inc, The Royal Tenebaums, Nightmare before Christmas, Moulin Rouge, Airplane!, Leon, Man on the moon, Lord of the rings.

Top filmmakers: Milos Foreman, Pixar, Studio Ghibli / Hayao Miyazaki, Baz Lhurmann.
