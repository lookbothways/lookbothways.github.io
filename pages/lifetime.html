2005 - to present Technical Director: Framestore-CFC
I am currently working at Framestore-CFC. My projects there include: The Golden Compass & Harry Potter and the order of the Phoenix. I was technical lead on ITV's sci-fi thriller 'Primeval' leading a team of 4 TD and was a rigging/lighting TD on the BBC docudrama 'Oceans Odyssey'.
2004 - 2005 Technical Director: Jim Henson's Creature Shop
I was at Henson's in Camden for a year where I worked on several TV and film projects including The Water Giant, Are we there yet? Francis and Pride. I was initially employed as a rigging TD using Maya, I then moved on to lookdev, lighting, texturing & fur in XSI.
2001 - 2003 3D artist: Uli Meyer Studios, Camden
I worked on every 3D project that came through during my time there. I used a very broad skillset that included modelling, lighting / multi pass rendering, rigging, animation, dynamics, tracking, technical direction and occasionally compositing.
1999 - 2000 Freelance artworker / web author: Bostock and Pollitt
My artworking experience proved essential at this medium-sized design company in Covent Garden. I illustrated brochures and created HTML pages, flash animation and some 3D modelling for clients such as n-power, Mappin & Webb and Princess Yachts.
1998 - 1999 Web author & advisor: Finlayson Design
This London design company had a few very talented designers, I brought my web and Photoshop skills to the team and maintained their Mac network.
1995 - 1997 Southampton Institute
I spent two years on a HND in Graphic Design at Southampton. I took a 3D illustration pathway (using Strata-Studio Pro - see left) and passed the final major project with Distinction. I spent most of my free-time with my flatmate on the internet - building homepages and occasionally computers, arranging LAN parties, raytracing spinning logo's and KPT Bryce landscapes (I had a raydiosity renderer that took forever).
1993 - 1995 Mid Warwickshire College of Art, Leamington Spa
I completed a two year B-tec in technical and information Illustration. Apart from design basics and all the natural-media we also covered multimedia, stop-frame animation, film editing and photography.
I thoroughly enjoyed this well-structured course, I came top of my year.
1988 - 1993 Henley in Arden High
I adored English, was in the top set for maths and loved all the sciences passionately. I played hockey when I wasn't in the computer room and was a prefect in my final year. Took 9 GCSE's A to C. Including English (A) and Maths (C).
April 1977
Born near London to an Art teacher and a Psychology Lecturer. Moved to Stratford Upon Avon aged seven.
