Eurovision Art Challenge

I run a monthly art competition via Uli's website.
The rules are simple, enter a Jpg on the theme, vote for your favourite in an anonymous poll and the winner gets to choose the next theme.
The layout is rough, but the entries are excellent. It has nothing to do with the song contest.
