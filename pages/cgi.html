I started with a copy of Swivel 3D in 1989, created lots of Bryce art and many, many spinning objects using Swivel 3D, Strata-Studio Pro, Cinema 4D, Infini-D and Poser.

After my B-tec in technical illustration I took a graphic design HND I took the 3D illustration pathway in the second year. More information about my education can be found in the 'Lifetime' secton here.
