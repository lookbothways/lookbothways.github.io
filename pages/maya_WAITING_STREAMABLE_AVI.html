The icons that appear throughout the showreel indicate which aspect of the scene I worked on. Effects includes particle and dynamic simulations including Maya Cloth and Fluids. I also did pre-vis, camera moves, tracking and pre-comp work on most scenes shown.

Below is a breakdown of some of that work, clicking a square will open a new window and a higher-res image. All work from Cookie Crisp and lower was created while at Uli Meyes Studios in Camden. All other work is from Jim Henson's Creature Shop.
BBC's 'The Pride'

Henson's created over 250 CG talking lion shots for this 2004 Christmas special. I was initialy rigging in Maya and then moved on to lighting and fur using XSI (Mental Ray). Most shots were tracked with minimal camera information. We removed whiskers, beards and jowels from the orginals and put it all back on. I also composited some of my scenes in Shake.
Cookie Crisp 'Slingshot' 'Spring in the City' and tags

I worked on two 30 second and two 10 second commercials. I built, textured and rendered the city and tracked the springs to the wolf and packs to the kids. The cookies are particle replacement geometry and in the latest tag - the red, dusky one - I used mental ray for the cookie dream bubble and pack shots. I tracked packs and springs, pinned and placed 2D characters, rendered multiple passes and cast 3D shadows of the 2D objects.
Skyactive 'FrankenRed'

The last of the SkyActive commercials, this was entirely rendered in Mental Ray. I textured and modelled some of the background props, lit several scenes, rendered RGB light passes and did the cloth simulation for scene four.
Skyactive 'Teens' & 'Banking'

Rendered using the 'Sky' plug-in for Maya. I made HDRI images for both commercials and created key lighting setups. I rigged Floyd the piggy bank with blend-shapes and smooth binding. I textured and remodelled the phone from a library model & lit and rendered many of the scenes in both commercials. I also tracked the camera plates from the live-action shots in Teens.
Skyactive 'Lara', 'High Noon' & 'Mayhem'

Also rendered with the 'Sky' plug-in. I built and textured the wall-sequence in Mayhem. Made the pre-vis museum and layout for Lara as well as lighting two scenes. Lit some of the close-up shots in High Noon, modelled and textured the Gamepad remote. I rigged, textured and animated the flipping magazine, rendered RGB and beauty passes and coordinated much of the pipeline in these three commercials.
Mr Sheen (2001)

I modelled and lit various CG packs for this live-action / traditional series of commercials. I also lit and rendered the 'floor-wipes' commercial, modelled the mop and made the cloth simulations, all of these were rendered using Maya's standard rendering engine.
Other work

I have also worked on projects for Honey Nut Cheerios, tracking and rendering packs into live plates and an intro for the German TV show 'Made in Europe', worked on several pitches and pilots, the pop video for the V-birds first single and a small project for Nickleodeon.
